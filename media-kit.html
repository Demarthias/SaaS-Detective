Why This Converts

Unlike banner ads or cold email, SaaS Detective shows your brand only when the user has already discovered you organically on a website they chose to visit. The user knows your tool exists, is curious about it, and is actively researching — making every click high-intent and self-qualified. This is the opposite of interruption marketing.

Promotion Method

Affiliate links are embedded in the extension's popup UI. When a user visits a website that uses a partner's product, a card for that product appears with a "Visit" button linked via a tracked affiliate URL. No cold audiences. No paid ads. Pure discovery-moment monetization.
